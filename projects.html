Featured Academic Research

B.Sc. Final Year Project: Multimodal Breast Cancer Detection

@ Landmark University (Dept. of Computer Science)

Research Scope: Addressed the limitations of single-modality screening by designing a system that combines Ultrasound Imaging with Clinical & Radiometric data.

Methodology & Tech Stack:
  • MedCLIP (Vision): Employed a vision-language model to extract semantic embeddings from ultrasound images, overcoming limited labeled data.
  • TabNet (Tabular): Utilized an attention-based network to process clinical metadata and radiometric features (area, solidity, etc.) extracted via regionprops.
  • Early Fusion Strategy: Concatenated 512-dim image embeddings with 64-dim tabular embeddings before classification to learn joint representations.
  • Performance: Achieved 82.7% Accuracy and 87.1% ROC-AUC, significantly outperforming unimodal baselines.
