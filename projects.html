FLAGSHIP PROJECT

B.Sc. Final Year Project: Multimodal Breast Cancer Detection

@ Landmark University — Department of Computer Science 2024 – 2025

Research Scope: Addressed the core limitation of single-modality breast cancer screening by designing a fusion architecture that combines heterogeneous data sources into a single, more accurate prediction model.

Methodology & Tech Stack:
  • MedCLIP (Vision): Extracted 512-dimensional semantic embeddings from ultrasound images using a vision-language model pretrained on medical image–text pairs.
  • TabNet (Tabular): Processed clinical metadata — patient age, BI-RADS score, tissue density, and radiometric features — using an attention-based encoder.
  • Early Fusion Strategy: Concatenated image + tabular embeddings into a unified 576-dim representation, passed through a fully connected classification head.
  • Results: 82.7% Accuracy • 87.1% ROC-AUC — significantly outperforming unimodal image-only and tabular-only baselines.
