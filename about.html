Meet the Founder

Bunmi Adelugba

Bunmi Adelugba is a seasoned financial professional with over 15 years of experience in audit, tax advisory, and corporate financial management across Nigeria’s private and public sectors.

A fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN), Bunmi founded BAC with a commitment to delivering financial services of the highest international standard, grounded in the realities of doing business in Nigeria.

Her approach combines rigorous technical expertise with deep commercial insight — helping clients not only achieve compliance, but build lasting financial strength. Under her leadership, BAC has served over 100 corporate clients spanning manufacturing, real estate, professional services, and the public sector.
